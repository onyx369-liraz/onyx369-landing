Frequently asked

Questions.

What is ONYX369?

ONYX369 is a SaaS platform that gives small businesses and marketing agencies AI-powered tools for content creation, lead generation, CRM, and client reporting. It replaces the marketing team you were going to hire.

How does billing work?

Monthly subscriptions billed through Stripe. No contracts, no setup fees. Cancel or change plans any time from your dashboard.

Is my data secure?

Yes. ONYX369 runs on Supabase with row-level security, meaning every client's data is fully isolated. All traffic is encrypted via Cloudflare and SSL. We never sell or share client data.

Do I need technical skills?

No. ONYX369 is built for business owners, not developers. If you can send an email, you can run the platform.

What kind of businesses is this for?

Service-based small businesses (contractors, fitness, real estate, restaurants, professional services) and the marketing agencies that serve them. Typical customers are 1–50 employees in the United States.
